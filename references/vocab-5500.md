> **完整词表**：见 [`vocab-5500-full.md`](vocab-5500-full.md) —— 共 5493 个词，按 A–Z 分组。出题前可 grep 单词确认是否在守界范围内。

## Boundary Rule (Iron Rule)

All reference translations and recommended vocabulary **must fall within the 5500-word CET-6 / Postgraduate Entrance Exam (考研) core vocabulary**. 

- Words outside the 5500 boundary: **do not use, do not recommend**.
- Borderline words (低频/边缘词): mark as `⚠️ 低频词汇，仅了解` and do not require the student to produce them.

## Checking Guidelines

1. **Prefer high-frequency words.** When multiple options exist, prioritize words with ★★★★☆ or ★★★★★ frequency in past exams (2010–2024).
2. **Mark borderline words.** If unsure whether a word is within the 5500 boundary, annotate it as `⚠️ 边缘词` in the output.
3. **Common pitfalls that push students out-of-bounds:**
   - Using GRE/TOEFL-level vocabulary for simple concepts (e.g., `ameliorate` instead of `improve`)
   - Using rare synonyms to show off (e.g., `pulchritudinous` instead of `beautiful`)
   - Directly translating Chinese idioms that have no 5500-level English equivalent
